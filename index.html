Cyber Security Fresher (Blue Team)

I am Nguyễn Minh Trung, a Cyber Security Fresher focused on SOC monitoring, network security, and practical defensive operations.

My work centers on turning security events into clear evidence: collecting firewall, IDS, Windows, Linux, and application logs; triaging alerts; correlating timestamps, IPs, ports, and protocols; and documenting findings so response work becomes faster and cleaner.

I am looking for internship or entry-level opportunities where I can contribute to log analysis, alert triage, incident investigation, IDS/IPS tuning, and system hardening. My long-term goal is to grow into a Security Engineer specializing in SOC operations, Blue Teaming, networking, and systems.
